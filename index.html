This is Smart India Hackathon 2019 winning project. The problem statement was given by Yamaha.

This application allows the user to scan the QR code of a bike to get its insights with the bike augmented on the screen.

This project was developed by a WHY NOTS team, consist of 6 members from St.Joseph's College of Engineering, Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
